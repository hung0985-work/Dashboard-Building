The purpose of this portfolio is to demonstrate my ability to transform raw business data into clear, interactive, and actionable dashboards that support performance monitoring, business analysis, and data-driven decision-making.

## 3. Dashboard Objectives

The dashboards in this portfolio were designed to support the following business needs:

- Track key business KPIs in an interactive and easy-to-understand format.
- Help users quickly monitor performance trends across different business areas.
- Provide visual insights for decision-making in insurance, marketing, sales, and reinsurance.
- Convert business data into dashboards that can be used by both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Improve reporting efficiency by replacing static reports with interactive BI dashboards.

## 6. How to Use This Repository

To view each dashboard:

1. Go to the **Dashboard Collection** section.
2. Click **View Dashboard** in the corresponding row.
3. Explore the interactive report using filters, charts, and KPI cards.
4. Review each dashboard based on its business domain and analytical purpose.

## 7. Dashboard Details

### 7.1. Insurance Analytics Dashboard

This dashboard focuses on insurance-related performance analysis. It helps users monitor key insurance metrics, understand policy/customer trends, and support business reporting in the insurance domain.

### 7.2. Marketing Campaign Dashboard

This dashboard is designed to evaluate marketing campaign performance. It supports analysis of campaign effectiveness, marketing channels, customer responses, and performance indicators.

### 7.3. Horizon Marketing Dashboard

This dashboard provides marketing-related insights for Horizon, focusing on marketing tracking, campaign visibility, and customer engagement patterns.

### 7.4. Company Sales Tracking Dashboard

This dashboard tracks company sales performance and business KPIs. It helps monitor revenue, sales trends, performance by category or period, and overall business growth.

### 7.5. Reinsurance Analytics Dashboard

This dashboard focuses on reinsurance analytics, supporting the monitoring of reinsurance performance, risk exposure, and key business indicators in the reinsurance field.
